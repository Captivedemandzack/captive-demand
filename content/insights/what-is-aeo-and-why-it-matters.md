Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so that AI systems — ChatGPT, Google SGE, Perplexity, and others — can find, understand, and cite you as a source. It's not a replacement for SEO; it's the next layer. Traditional SEO gets you on page one of Google. AEO gets you into the answer itself.

## Why AEO Matters Now

Search is shifting. Users are increasingly asking AI assistants questions instead of typing keywords into Google. When someone asks "What's the best CBD brand for sleep?" in ChatGPT, the model doesn't return ten blue links. It returns a synthesized answer. Your brand either gets cited or it doesn't. There's no page two.

The brands that win in this environment are the ones whose content is structured for machine comprehension. Clear entity relationships, explicit answers to common questions, and content that reads like a reference source rather than a sales pitch.

## How to Start

Start with your existing content. Audit what you have. Identify the questions your audience actually asks. Then restructure your answers so they're unambiguous, scannable, and cite-worthy. Use schema markup. Write in a way that both humans and models can parse. The brands doing this today are building a moat that gets wider every quarter.
