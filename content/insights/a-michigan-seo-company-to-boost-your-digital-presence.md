## Understanding SEO and Its Impact

Search engine optimization is the process of improving your website to make it more visible on search engine result pages (SERPs). By optimizing your site for relevant keywords and phrases, you can improve your chances of ranking higher and driving organic traffic to your site.

There are many different SEO strategies, ranging from on-page optimization to link building. On-page optimization is an important part of SEO as it helps search engines like Google understand what the page is about and how relevant it is for certain searches.

Meanwhile, link building helps to increase the authority of a website by creating backlinks from external sites that point to your content. This shows search engines that other websites have found your content to be valuable and trustworthy.
